How to see hysteresis
1.Iw+ is on (green). At Ieff = 18 mV the cell is silent — below rheobase Ion = Vθ−Vrest = 20 mV. Operating point on the lower branch.
2.Select +rect and click Fire — the pulse pushes V over Vθ; each spike adds b+ to Iw+, which sustains firing. The state jumps to the upper branch.
3.Slowly drag Ieff down — spiking persists below Ion, all the way to Ioff. Then it collapses.
4.Raise Ieff — stays silent until Ion. Same input, two states: hysteresis.
★Toggle Iw+ off to recover the plain LIF (no bistability). K = b+·τw+/1000 mV/Hz sets the load-line slope.
