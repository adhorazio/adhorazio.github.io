How to see hysteresis
1.At Ieff = 50 µA the system is silent — below Ion ≈ 52.6 µA without Iw+. Operating point on the lower (blue) branch.
2.Select +rect stimulus and click Fire — Iw+ pushes Inet above Ion. Persistent spiking builds to the upper (green) branch.
3.Slowly drag Ieff down — spiking persists below Ion, all the way to Ioff (orange). Then it collapses.
4.Raise Ieff — stays silent until Ion (yellow). Same current, different state: hysteresis.
★Pure LIF: f → 0 at Ion (type-I onset). K = b+·τw+/1000 µA/Hz. Click Auto-scan to trace the hysteresis loop.
